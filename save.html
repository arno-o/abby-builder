What do I use my identity for?

Your identity helps make your Abby experience more personal. It’s visible in live installations, shows up in community spaces, and lets others get a glimpse of what makes you, you. You can build it from scratch, or keep shaping it each time you visit.

The 'Abby You' App

The Abby app lets you be part of the community. You can suggest ideas, vote on what happens next, and join in shaping the space and its programme. It shows what’s going on live at Abby: how busy it is, what’s coming up, and who’s there. You can also keep track of your Abby Identity as it grows with each visit and interaction.
